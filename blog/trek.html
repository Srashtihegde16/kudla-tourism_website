Trekking sites around Mangalore

Mangalore, located in the southwestern state of Karnataka in India, is a beautiful city that offers a variety of trekking opportunities for outdoor enthusiasts. Here are some of the best treks around Mangalores

Explore

1. KumaraParvatha

Kumara Parvatha is another popular trekking destination located around 130 km from Mangalore. The trek to the peak is a challenging one, and it takes you through dense forests, grasslands, and steep slopes. The trek offers stunning views of the Western Ghats and the surrounding valleys.

2. Kudremukha

Kudremukh is a popular trekking destination located around 100 km from Mangalore. The trek to the peak is a challenging one, but the stunning views of the Western Ghats make it worth the effort. The trek starts from a small village called Mullodi and takes you through lush green forests, waterfalls, and grasslands before reaching the summit.

3. Ranipuram

In the Kasargod district of Kerala is Ranipuram, popularly known as the ‘Ooty of Kerala’ and previously called Madathumala. It is an offbeat trekking destination, which is sparsely populated and provides panoramic views as you walk across misty, lush green grasslands. Due to the length of the trail, Ranipuram makes for a perfect one-day getaway if you reside in or around Mangalore or in the northern part of Kerala.

4. Mullayanagiri

Mullayanagiri is the highest peak in Karnataka, located around 160 km from Mangalore. The trek to the summit is a moderate one and takes you through coffee plantations, dense forests, and grasslands. The views from the top are stunning, and you can see the surrounding valleys and hills.

5. Narasimha Parvatha

Narasimha Parvatha is the highest peak in Agumbe. Standing at a height of 3,772 ft, the experience this trek offers is wholesome. It takes you through the canopy of dense, tall trees of Western Ghats. It also takes you through hills and valleys covered in a blanket of lush green grasslands. On top of it, the views it offers are spectacular, to say the least.

6. Tadiyandamol

Tadiandamol is the highest peak in Coorg, located around 140 km from Mangalore. The trek to the summit is a moderate one and takes you through coffee plantations, dense forests, and grasslands. The views from the top are breathtaking, and you can see the entire Coorg region from there.
